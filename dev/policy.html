API Policy

We hope to keep Slambooke's API as open as possible. This means free access without registering apps and so forth. To make this possible your app, service or library needs to respect our policies.

  • Do not abuse the API or use it excessively. If you're unsure whether your planned use is excessive, email us.
  • Make sure your app/service/hack can't be mistaken as an official app. This includes app naming:
    • Don't use the name Slambooke in your application, url or branding unless it's clearly separated (i.e. "Exercisebooke for Slambooke" is ok but "RSS for Slambooke" can be confusing).
    • Slambooke's assets (logos, code) including that of schools are copyrighted, don't use them without explicit permission.
  • Never store the schools information provied by the api.
  • Schools retain the right to their content, it can't be redistributed or sold.
  • Slambooke may terminate your license to the Slambooke API under these terms at any time for any reason.
  • Slambooke reserves the right to update and change these terms from time to time without notice.
