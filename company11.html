What is Slambooke?
Slambooke is a web application that organizes schools information and make it universally accessible and useful.

Our Mission:
Our mission is to organize schools information and make it universally accessible and useful.

Our Vision:
Our vision is to make schools more open and connected as well as make their informations universally accessible and useful to everyone in the world.
