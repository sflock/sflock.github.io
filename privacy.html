Data Use Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Slambooke we have a few fundamental principles:

       1) We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.

       2) We don’t share your personal information with anyone.

       3) We don’t store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of one of our services.


If you have questions about deleting or correcting your personal data please contact our support team.

Slambooke operates the website slambooke.com. It is Slambooke’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our websites.


Aggregated Statistics

Slambooke may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its websites. For instance, Slambooke may monitor the most popular links or screen the links help identify spam. Slambooke may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Slambooke does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below.


Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Slambooke may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in Slambooke’s sole discretion. Slambooke encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
